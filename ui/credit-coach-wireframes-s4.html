Screen 5: Data Export (DSAR) /credit-coach/settings/export NEW
← Export My Data
Download your credit coaching data

Under UK GDPR (Article 15), you can request a copy of all data we hold about you in the Credit Coach.

Your export will include:
• Score history (all months)
• Score factors at each refresh
• Improvement plans and actions
• Simulation history
• Offers presented and your responses
• Alert history
• Consent records
Format: JSON (machine-readable) + PDF summary
Delivery: Available within 24 hours (usually minutes)
Legal deadline: 1 calendar month per GDPR Art. 15

Regulatory Compliance Notes

  • GDPR Art. 17 (Right to Erasure): Screen 3 — clear explanation of what's deleted vs retained (CCA exemptions)
  • GDPR Art. 22 (Automated Decisions): Screen 4 — meaningful info about logic, right to human review, right to contest
  • GDPR Art. 15 (Right of Access / DSAR): Screen 5 — export in machine-readable format within 1 month
  • DUAA 2025: Screen 4 — high-risk ADM safeguards for credit decisions
  • Debt Respite Scheme 2021: Screen 8 — 60-day moratorium, interest frozen, offers suppressed
  • CONC 7.3: Screen 8 — debt advice signposting (StepChange, Citizens Advice, National Debtline)
  • FCA Consumer Duty: Screen 6 — human escalation available from any screen (consumer support outcome)
  • PRA FS2/23: Screen 4 — explainability of automated credit decisions
  • CRA Score Scales: Screen 7 — Experian (0–1,250), Equifax (0–1,000), TransUnion (0–710) per enterprise architecture KB Section 9.1
